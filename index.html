About Ryan

I am Ryan Hirst, a student, gamer, soccer enthusiast, and amateur cook from Kalamazoo, Michigan. I'm currently studying and living in East Lansing, MI, where I'll be graduating from Michigan State University in May of 2015 with a major in Media & Information and a minor in Computer Science. School usually keeps me pretty busy, but the free time I get is usually filled with mocking up new designs for my websites and coding new web apps and games.

Before making my way to the great city of East Lansing, I attended high school in the small town of Mattawan, Michigan, at the aptly named Mattawan High School.
